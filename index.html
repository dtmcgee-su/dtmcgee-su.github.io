About me

Major: Sport Analytics
Minor: Information Management and Technology
College: Syracuse University class of 2021

Like most kids, I spent most of my days dreaming the day I could say “I play professional baseball.” My whole life, I had prepared myself to take small steps towards that goal. But for the first time, it felt as if I was walking backward. After playing two seasons at my local community college, I had a choice to make. Do I continue to chase my dream by playing baseball at a four-year college, which won’t satisfy me, academically? Or do I take a risk, quit the game I love, and give the prestigious Sports Analytics degree at Syracuse University a chance? The outcome may not have been what I dreamed about as a little kid, but I believe everything happens for a reason. My passion for the game, the college decisions I made, and the people I met lead me into my true passion; analytics. As I walked through the halls of Syracuse University, I realized it was my turn to quit the children’s game. With my strength in math and passion for statistics, I soon realized that “the game” was beginning for me.
